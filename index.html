Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to rent?

You need a valid driver's license and a government-issued ID. International drivers may need an International Driving Permit.

Is there a security deposit?

Yes, a small refundable security deposit is required at the time of pickup to cover incidentals.

What is the fuel policy?

We operate on a 'Full-to-Full' policy. You receive the car with a full tank and should return it the same way.
